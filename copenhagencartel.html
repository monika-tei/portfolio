Copenhagen Cartel project

A 2nd semester project with local sustainable swimwear brand Copenhagen Cartel. For this case project, we heard our clients wishes to understand potential younger target audience and possible solutions for their content approach. We worked in groups to

  • User Research (interviews), Trend Analysis on Instagram & TikTok
  • Downloading, Synthesizing and creating insights from User Interviews
  • Moodboards, Prototyping of website, SoMe content
  • Creating a concept, customer journey and content strategy
  • Website, SoMe content and video
  • Working with client brief and pitching back the solution
