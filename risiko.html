Risiko magazine: Assistant Editor / Translator

RISIKO is a Berlin-based independent magazine that covers current German underground and alternative music scene. Genres such as Krautrock, Neue Deutsche Welle, the Hamburger Schule and music from the present day. During 2 years of collaboration, I took part in contributing to the debut Issue 1: "The Wall" and Issue 2: "Krautie"

  • Translation of articles & interviews from Japanese to English
  • Proofreading, fact-checking
  • Led communication with contributing artists and potential Ad-partners
  • Created a kickstarter campaign for Issue 1
  • Helped shape the vision of the magazine and its production
